About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments.

IDR bridges academic insight and real-world practice through partnerships with universities and industry organisations.

Our Model

Academy

Training programs and bootcamps that prepare students and professionals for careers in digital risk, cybersecurity and AI governance.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing intellectual property, future risk models and AI governance frameworks to support digital resilience.

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2 for effective risk management.

Who We Serve

IDR supports students, early-career professionals and industry practitioners in sectors such as financial services and critical infrastructure who want to build expertise in digital and cyber risk.
